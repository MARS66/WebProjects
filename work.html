How Does ASO Work?

While the perfect ASO technique may vary, they all involve the process of improving various elements of an app’s submission details and marketing techniques to help achieve a higher ranking in the search results of Apple and Google’s App Stores. This in turn achieves better app visibility , discoverability , and conversions.

67%

With 67% of users reporting that the last app they downloaded was found via an app store search, learning how to improve search ranking within those stores has become one of the most critical publisher ASO requirements.

Paid installs (mobile advertising campaigns) drive organic installs. On average, apps generate 1.5 organic installs for every paid install which can exponentially grow to rates of 3x - 10x if you achieve top chart rankings.

How Does ASO Work?

The algorithms used by the app stores heavily favour downloads. A study completed by Tune on thousands of apps across both the Google Play Store and the Apple App Store found that for every paid install an app receives, it can expect to see an additional 1.5 organic installs (1.5 being the “organic multiplier”). Apps that achieve top chart rankings have been seen to have their organic multiplier grow from 3x to 10x, we call this the ‘halo’ effect. Because downloads can both directly and indirectly influence how the motivation types mentioned above learn about apps and how to find them, it is important to understand the ways App Store Optimization (ASO) can maximize your organic install multiplier to exploit this effec

Why ASO should be central to your marketing strategy

Developers and Advertisers will usually deploy ASO tools to get their on-page factors fine-tuned. It is very common to see a disregard for off-page factors because they are very difficult to influence. It is vital to note that by ignoring off-page factors, publishers will likely never reach the top 3 positions which is to their detriment since over 50% of keyword traffic goes to the top 3 listings. The visibility of your app in search ranking is very important. 82% of users never browse past the top 25 search results in the app store.

Share of installs related to keyword traffic

  • 30% of installs typically occur to the app in position #1,
  • 17% in position #2,
  • 12% in position #3,
  • ...and outside of the top 10 it’s close to 0.10%

Improving your search rankings directly impacts the increasing rate of your organic installs. While the actual ranking algorithm is a heavily guarded secret, proper research can help us reasonably understand the factors that influence their value.
