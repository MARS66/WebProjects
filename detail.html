The recent crypto bull market behind the alt data set

2021-03-27

In fact, when you look at other traditional commodities such as gold, or indexes such as S and P 500, you can see that Bitcoin's gains have almost tripled this year alone.　　 Why?　　 This is the most important factor: 　　 Pandemic flu panic-unemployment rate is rolling, social distance affects the uncertainty of the industry's long-term prospects, and fiat currency inflation concerns, investors are eager for commodities that can help store value.　　 Institutional interest-Alt-coins, especially Bitcoin, began to be of interest to major institutions. The most notable is Tesla's purchase of $1.5 billion in Bitcoin, which was jointly established with Vantone Financial Group to commend the investment of $100 million.　　 Payment methods-Many leading companies have begun to accept payments in crypto, including Starbucks, PayPal, and Etsy. This is widely used in the retail industry to actively promote circulation and affect prices. Both can help you identify movements driven by asset valuation factors. This type of data can help your team track whether social indicators indicate bullish or bearish sentiment, and inform buy/sell/hold decisions. For example, if you are collecting social mentions of Etherium on a given whale account, on average you see some 20 mentions of coins every day. On a certain day, you may mention the 30th pick-up or a 50% surge in whale interest, achieving an increase in Etherium's valuation. Once these are established, "social signals" can help trigger a buy/sell order before any major migration (whether it is a buy or sell). One can also collect other data sets with these estimates and create deductive models of changes. For example, suppose you are collecting data on the value of bitcoins and you realize that the price has increased to 44,200 dollars. The fact that you can collect news data points at the same time will result in a $1.5 billion investment in many of your works about Tesla in such assets. This may cause your analysts to set up trigger trading models based on the purchase of alt-coins by large institutional investors or corporate entities.
