IOS APP STORE

OPTIMIZING MOBILE APPS TO RANK HIGHER IN AN APP STORE’S SEARCH RESULTS IS KNOWN AS APP STORE OPTIMIZATION (ASO). THE HIGHER AN APP RANKS IN AN APP STORE’S SEARCH RESULTS, THE EASIER IT IS FOR POTENTIAL USERS TO DISCOVER AND DOWNLOAD THE APP.

    Download Velocity: How many downloads are achieved in a 7 day period, and a 30 day period are both considered, but the most recent 7 day period is more heavily weighted.

  • 02

    Total Downloads: The total number of downloads for your app are considered, but downloads have a decaying factor; as the volume of ‘recent downloads’ increases, the weight of their impact on the rankings multiples. Thus, download velocity is even more critical to achieve Play Store ranking success than the total # of downloads.
